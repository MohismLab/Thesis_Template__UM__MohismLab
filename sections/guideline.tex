\begin{enumerate}[nosep, leftmargin=*] % nosep 让列表非常紧凑
	\item The report should be type-written in English in the \textbf{THIRD PERSON} in double line spacing.
	
	\item The content of the report should be in the following order:
	\begin{enumerate}[nosep]
		\item Cover page
		\item Title page
		\item Abstract
		\item Table of contents
		\item List of figures (if any)
		\item List of tables (if any)
		\item List of abbreviations (if any)
		\item Acknowledgements
		\item Main body
		\item References \\
		A reference section is a required component. Failure to citation of prior works may be interpreted as claiming those works to be your own. Plagiarism is one of the most serious offences.
		\item Appendices (if any)
		\item Work Breakdown
	\end{enumerate}
	\vspace{1.5em}
	\item The other format details are shown below:
	\vspace{-0.5em}
	\begin{center}
		\renewcommand{\arraystretch}{1.2} % 如果觉得表格太挤，可以取消注释这行
		\begin{tabular}{|p{0.32\textwidth}|p{0.58\textwidth}|}
			\hline
			Paper Size & A4 \\
			\hline
			Line Spacing & Double Spacing \\
			\hline
			Top and Bottom Margins & 25 mm \\
			\hline
			Left Margins & 32 mm \\
			\hline
			Right Margins & 25 mm \\
			\hline
			Font & Times New Roman \\
			\hline
			Main Text Font Size & 12 points \\
			\hline
			Chapter Heading Font Size & 14 points, in Capital \\
			\hline
			Section Heading Font Size & 12 points, in Capital \\
			\hline
			Tables / Figures & Consecutively numbered with titles above for tables and below for figures. \\
			\hline
			Equations & Centered and numbered consecutively. Equation numbers appear at the extreme right of text in parentheses. Equations are referred as Eq. (1), Eq. (2) in text.  \\
			\hline
		\end{tabular}
	\end{center}
	
	
	\item The hard copy should be printed as single sided.
	
	\item Students should strictly follow the report format; otherwise assessment will not be carried out.
	
	\item Students are reminded to take note of data integrity. Data fabrication is a serious misconduct.
\end{enumerate}
